Project Overview

Our team has developed an advanced robotic solution capable of autonomously de-icing and inspecting high-voltage power lines, enhancing operational safety and efficiency in energy transmission systems. This project addresses the critical need for maintaining China's expansive energy infrastructure, particularly in challenging environmental conditions.

Key Features

  • Biomimetic design inspired by gibbon movements for agile navigation
  • Advanced mechanical structure for precise de-icing operations
  • AI and computer vision for real-time obstacle detection and decision-making
  • IoT integration for remote monitoring and control

Development Process

  1. Created comprehensive 3D mechanical model
  2. Implemented YOLO for image segmentation and obstacle recognition
  3. Conducted extensive field tests to refine functionalities
  4. Integrated IoT capabilities for real-time data streaming

Achievements

Our project secured a prestigious award at the "Hui Le Technology Cup", demonstrating its innovation and potential impact on the industry. This recognition validates our approach and highlights the significance of our solution in addressing real-world challenges in power infrastructure maintenance.

Future Directions

We plan to enhance the robot's design for improved balance and obstacle navigation, incorporating additional support mechanisms and adjusting the center of gravity to optimize performance and extend operational capabilities.
